Guys many of you heard about the term "core company" ,but do you know what it exactly mean ?Let me explain , Any company which makes use of what you've learned in your graduation are better considered as Core Company.Now for instance, If you are a mechanical engineer you would apply the things you learnt during your academics on your job.For better understanding you may apply the designing skills you've learnt in solidworks,catia and so on.Similar to thermal engineers they would apply their calculative experience and engine knowledge on their job.  Here the list of core companies for mechanical engineers are chronicled ,       First let we split the Core Companies to two types :                    Public Sector Undertakings is mostly known as PSU and                         Private Sector.
