Electric Vehicles is the heart of Automotive Industry . Many people from Mechanical Engineering have a passion to get placed in Automobile sector . Now the Industry is becoming wider and there will be a huge demand for engineers in this particular field . But you are required some special skills to work in this trending field , because it is incorporated with different specialization . You should be master at least one of the below skills to unleash your dreams . The Skills which would make you the best to suit the profile are categorized below , Make a note and start developing your skills . 

 
Battery Management System :
 
You should develop your skills on learning about circuits and basics such as voltage , optimum power input , capacity of a battery . You should able to handle a battery with more conscious  about its hazardous effects . I guess you caught that. 

Design :
 
Design is a major aspect to build a battery . The battery's physical aspects should match with its application device requirements . You don't need to be a pro in designer but should have some knowledge on electronics too because you are going to design considering that factor too .

  • Control System 
  • Software and Programming Skills 
  • Lean Manufacturing 
  • Research and Development 
  • Artificial Intelligence 
  • Robotics 
  • Embedded Devices 
  • Green Manufacturing 
  • Power Electronics
